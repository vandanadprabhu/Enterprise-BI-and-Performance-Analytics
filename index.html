Business Highlights

India generated the highest overall revenue across all operating regions.
Pharma is the top revenue-generating department, but also carries the highest financial risk exposure.
Technology achieved the strongest profit margin, indicating the most efficient operations.
Analytics Service contributed the largest share of product revenue.
Overall business profit margin remained at approximately 48.38%, indicating healthy financial performance.

Recommended Actions

Expand investment in India. Increase investment in high-performing regions such as India.
Strengthen risk controls in Pharma. Reduce financial exposure in Pharma through improved risk management.
Standardize Technology's operating practices. Extend its operational efficiencies across other departments.
Continue investment in Analytics Service. Sustain growth given its strong revenue contribution.

About

Vandana Prabhu is an MSc Organic Chemistry student working at the intersection of pharmaceutical analytics, cheminformatics and applied machine learning. Her technical background includes Python, RDKit, SQL and Power BI, developed through applied projects such as an AQbD-based Critical Variable Predictor built during an industry internship, alongside coursework in molecular docking and drug discovery pipelines.

This project presents an end-to-end MySQL and Power BI analysis of enterprise financial and risk data, structured to demonstrate a complete analytics workflow: from raw data through SQL-based analysis to an executive-ready reporting layer.
